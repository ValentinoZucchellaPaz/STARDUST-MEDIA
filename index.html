About us

I, Maria Luz Zucchella, have been working with content for over 22 years, in particular in US Latino market- but I’ve handled content acquisitions for different platforms and channels, and for other markets as well.
Stardust Media have successfully consulted for OTT content aggregators and platforms. We specialize in helping start-ups by providing a one-stop-shop content solution.
As an example, we have consulted for companies such as Canela.tv / Canela Media since their launch and until early April this year- doing all things content -from acquisitions to processes. Canela recently got funded 32M.
We identified, negotiated, and licensed over 95% of all content on air, as well as adding most 3rd party FAST channels. We have set up their programming department, helped hire people on content, programming and ops; helped create and streamline processes; worked with sales on sponsor deals- and started working on originals’ production.
We did all the content work for an SVOD that failed to launch in US; and we are currently working for an SVOD that will launch shortly in Mexico.
In the past we have also consulted for one of the leading content aggregators, worked directly with Prime dashboard. Their FAST is currently top 10 on SamsungTV+.
Prior to starting Stardust Media I have worked for a US-Latino TV company for almost 20 years as VP content acquisitions: I was personally involved on all new projects launched over 10 networks in US, 4 networks in Middle East and N Africa, started their original productions department.
Recently, we had included subtitle and CC edition and creation services, providing a top quality subtitle product. We had worked for many latin and canadian larger companies, having subtitled more than 50,000 hours total.
Our latest investment was the production of the mexican movie "Cosas de adultos". This was the first step of many more to come for the company in producing movies.
